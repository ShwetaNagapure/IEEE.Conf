About the Conference

The **International Conference on Multidisciplinary Innovations for Smart & Sustainable Future (MISSF-2026)** aims to provide a global platform for researchers and industry professionals to exchange knowledge in the fields of multidisciplinary technologies. The conference seeks to foster discussions on theoretical and practical advancements in sustainable development.

Conference Themes

  • Technological Innovations in Renewable Energy
  • Social and Economic Impacts of Renewable Energy
  • AI-Driven Renewable Energy Forecasting
  • Energy Consumption Optimization
  • Smart Grid Technologies
  • AI-Powered Climate Risk Assessment
  • Waste Management and Recycling Optimization
  • AI for Water Resource Management

About SVKM’s Institute of Technology

SVKM’s Dhule campus is emerging as a major educational and technology hub in India. Located at the junction of three National Highways (NH-3, NH-6, NH-211), Dhule is poised for industrial and infrastructural growth. SVKM has taken an initiative to develop a state-of-the-art institute providing quality education in engineering and technology.
