Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original research papers that are **not published** or **under consideration elsewhere**.

  • All papers must follow the **IEEE conference template**.
  • Submissions must be in **PDF format** and should not exceed **6 pages**.
  • Papers should be **written in English** and formatted according to the **IEEE two-column format**.
  • All submissions will be **double-blind peer-reviewed**.
  • Accepted papers will be published in the **conference proceedings**.

Submission Process

To submit your paper, follow these steps:

  1. Prepare your paper using the IEEE Paper Template.
  2. Convert your document into **PDF format**.
  3. Go to the EasyChair Submission Portal.
  4. Login or create an EasyChair account.
  5. Upload your paper and complete the submission form.
